Introduction

Independent living is about learning the skills you need to live your life in a way that works for you. Everyone is different and you don’t have to do everything on your own.

This preparedness calculator helps you see what you can already do, where you might need support and what you want for the future. There are no right or wrong answers – it’s about making sure you feel ready and so that you know where you can get the right support.

Instructions
  • Tick each step you can do. This will give you a Red, Amber, Green rating for each section.
  • Red: Think about who/what can help you with these steps. Scan the QR codes for resources.
  • Amber: Think about what you need to do next on the list.
  • Green: Reflect on what you do that makes you feel confident in this area.
